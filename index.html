From first conversation to live system — here is exactly what working with us looks like.

No vague roadmaps. No 90-day discovery phases that produce a document. We move fast, we stay close, and we tell you when something will not work before you spend money on it.

01

Understanding call — 30 minutes

We ask about the problem, not the product preference. What is breaking? What is slow? What has already been tried? We leave this call with a clear sense of whether we are the right fit and whether the timing makes sense.

02

Assessment — 1 to 2 weeks

For AI projects: a process and data audit. For Zoho: a system and workflow review. We come in, we look at what is actually happening, and we produce a short, honest document that tells you what is worth building and what it will take.

03

Build and deploy — 4 to 8 weeks

We work in short cycles with your team in the room. No disappearing for three months and returning with something you did not ask for. You see progress weekly. You give feedback. The system is shaped by your reality.

04

Handover and support

We do not hand over a manual and disappear. Your team gets trained. The system is documented. And we stay available — because the first 60 days in production are where most AI projects succeed or quietly fail.
