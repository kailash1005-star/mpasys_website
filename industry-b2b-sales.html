The pressure points

Three reasons a sales team stops trusting its own CRM.

Recognise any of these? They are configuration and workflow problems — and every one of them is fixable.

Stale leads & dirty data

Duplicate records, missing fields and untouched leads mean nobody trusts the numbers — so reps keep their real pipeline in a private spreadsheet.

Manual updates eating selling time

Logging activity, chasing next steps and updating stages by hand is the work reps skip first — leaving the pipeline perpetually out of date.

No pipeline clarity

Without clear stages and reliable data, forecasting is guesswork and managers cannot see which deals are real and which are wishful thinking.
