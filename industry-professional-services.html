Your industry

Your most valuable people spend too much time on work only they can review — but anyone could prepare.

In professional services, margin lives in senior judgement. Yet partners and principals lose hours to first drafts, document review, research and admin that does not need their expertise to produce — only to check.

We build AI that does the preparation so your experts do the deciding. They keep ownership and quality; they just get there faster, on more matters, with less burnout.

The pressure points

Three places where expertise gets spent on the wrong work.

Every firm we work with recognises these. They are not talent problems — they are leverage problems, and leverage is buildable.

Senior time on first drafts

Partners writing the first version of documents, memos and proposals that an assistant layer could prepare — leaving review, the high-value step, squeezed.

Slow document & contract review

Manual reading of long contracts and files where the relevant clauses could be surfaced in minutes, freeing experts to focus on the judgement calls.

Knowledge locked in people's heads

Precedents, past matters and house style live in individual inboxes, so newer staff reinvent work the firm has already done many times.
