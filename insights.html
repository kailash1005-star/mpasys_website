How we engage

From first conversation to live system — four steps, no surprises.

We move fast, we stay close, and we tell you when something will not work before you spend money on it. Each step has a clear purpose and a clear end.

01Understanding call · 30 minutes

Understanding call — 30 minutes

We ask about the problem, not the product preference. What is breaking? What is slow? What has already been tried? We leave this call with a clear sense of whether we are the right fit and whether the timing makes sense — and we will tell you honestly if we are not.

No sales deckProblem-firstHonest fit check
02Assessment · 1 to 2 weeks

Assessment — 1 to 2 weeks

For AI projects: a process and data audit. For Zoho: a system and workflow review. We come in, we look at what is actually happening, and we produce a short, honest document that tells you what is worth building, what it will take, and — just as important — what to leave alone for now.

Process & data audit3–5 use casesWritten, honest scope
03Build & deploy · 4 to 8 weeks

Build and deploy — 4 to 8 weeks

We work in short cycles with your team in the room. No disappearing for three months and returning with something you did not ask for. You see progress weekly. You give feedback. The system is shaped by your reality — and every data flow is documented so compliance is never an afterthought.

Weekly progressGDPR by designProduction, not a demo
04Handover & support

Handover and support

We do not hand over a manual and disappear. Your team gets trained. The system is documented. And we stay available — because the first 60 days in production are where most AI projects succeed or quietly fail, and that is exactly when a partner should still be close.

Team trainingFull documentationFirst-60-days support
