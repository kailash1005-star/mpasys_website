Your industry

In a regulated business, an AI you cannot explain is a liability — not an asset.

The opportunity in finance and insurance is real: faster underwriting prep, quicker document review, less manual reconciliation. The risk is equally real if the system cannot show how it reached a decision.

We architect from compliance, not toward it. Every data flow is documented, every output is traceable, and access is controlled by design — so you can move faster and still answer to your regulator.

The pressure points

Three constraints that make AI in finance harder than it looks.

These are the reasons most financial-services AI projects stall at the compliance review. We design for them from the start.

Explainability requirements

A black-box model that cannot justify an outcome is a non-starter when an auditor, a regulator or a customer asks why a decision was made.

Sensitive data, strict access

Personal and financial data carries hard obligations on residency, access and retention — and a single careless integration creates real exposure.

Manual document & reconciliation work

Skilled staff spend hours on document review, data entry and reconciliation that is repetitive enough to automate but sensitive enough to need control.

How we turn it around

AI and Zoho built for audit — controllable, traceable, explainable.

01

Explainable document AI

LLM workflows for policy, claims and contract review that show their sources and reasoning — so a reviewer can verify every extraction instead of trusting a black box.

02

Controlled-access architecture

Systems designed around data residency, role-based access and retention rules from day one, with every data flow documented for your legal and compliance teams.

03

Auditable automation

Reconciliation, classification and routing automated with full audit trails — you keep human sign-off where it matters, the system handles the repetitive volume.

04

Zoho for regulated operations

A CRM and back-office configured with the controls your sector requires — clean data, consent handling, and integrations that keep sensitive information where it belongs.
